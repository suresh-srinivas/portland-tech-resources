Portland Tech Transition Resources

Curated support for Intel alumni and other tech professionals navigating career transitions in the Portland metro area.

Re-Launch • Re-Skill • Re-Connect

Join us for a half-day meetup in July crafted for professionals charting their post-Intel / post-Nike path—and for startups and fresh grads searching for seasoned talent and guidance.

Career Track (first 2 hrs) – one-on-one résumé reviews, AI-upskilling crash courses, design-thinking workshop, and sector-savvy job leads.

Future-Forward Track (last 2 hrs) – mingle with founders who need execs-in-residence or mentors, plus recent graduates eager to contribute.

Expect lightning talks, a Job & Startup Fair, honest conversations about “life after the badge,” and plenty of networking over sponsored food and parking.

Complete this quick survey to reserve your seat—first 50 respondents get priority résumé critiques. Bring curiosity, an updated résumé, and a willingness to ask & offer help. Let’s turn uncertainty into your next chapter, together.
